KA AWA ONA KAU LAAU O PUNA.
1 makemake oe i ka Awa ona ea, e hiki no ke hoolawaia aku kou iiui ke ike ae ia Mrs. Ane Kaea ma ka Makeko Kuai l'a ma ka aoao ma Waikiki, ina ka Rumi llelu 33. He olnolu na kuka 'i olelo ana, he waipahe hoi ua kamailio ana, o ka naue mai no i p&u kuhihowa, he ole ka lone pepeiao. 19. 126. \ug. 2. P.
